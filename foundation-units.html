How to split between two people

  • Default to equal unless one person clearly contributed more capital, IP, or full-time months.
  • Carve out 10–20% treasury before splitting the remainder — avoids renegotiating later.
  • Vest founder tokens — 4 years / 1-year cliff protects both parties.
  • Write it down — copy the genesis ledger and attach it to a founders' agreement.
